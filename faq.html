Frequently Asked Questions

  1. I have specific ideas for my photo shoot. Do you have a lot of props to use in the pictures?

  2. I'm not sure that I have everything that I might need for the shoot. Do you have a checklist?

    -Yes, we do. Please bring any individual items that you want to include in the shoots, e.g wedding rings for fiances, tiara for toddler b-day shoot, etc. Please bring at least 1 extra change of clothes for all children in group. Also remember to bring an external drive for us to use to send you the pics when we're done.
